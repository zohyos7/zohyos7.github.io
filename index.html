Biography

Yoonseo Zoh is currently working as a Research Assistant in CCS Lab. She received her B.A. in Psychology and Education from Seoul National University with summa cum laude. She is broadly interested in the neural mechanisms underlying cognitive and affective processes in social contexts. Her research interests include moral decision making, empathy and prosocial behavior. In the CCS Lab, Yoonseo hopes to gain knowledge in computational modeling and neuroimaging methods.
