Final Project of Computational Modeling Class, Spring 2018

Yoonseo Zoh , Hyeonjin Kim
Jun 8, 2018

Hyeonjin and I’s final project on Computational Modeling Class by Dr.Woo-Young Ahn, Spring 2018. We tried to replicate Leong & Zaki(2017) by using the same data and models, but instead with Bayesian Hierarchical modeling. We also proposed new hybrid model of WSLS-RL and carried out model comparison, parameter recovery, and posterior predictive check.
